About

Nathan Stier is a mobile and web developer from Madison, WI. He has two Associates Degrees from Madison College: IT - Web Programmer Analyst and IT - Mobile Applications Developer. He currently works at Madison Newspapers, Inc. developing applications for the iPhone as well as Android. He also does work under Lonely Robot Studio as the developer side of a designer-developer team. Outside of work he practices web development using Node.js and Ruby on Rails. In his spare time he can be found dying in Dark Souls, punching wood on his Minecraft server, practicing social dance at Fred Astaire Dance Studio in Madison, or rolling dice while making spreadsheets in a game of Dungeons and Dragons.
